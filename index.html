rockiscope

A Bluesky bot that posts the daily horoscope and game prediction for the Colorado Rockies. The Rockies are a Cancer (founded July 5, 1991), so the bot scrapes the daily Cancer horoscope and combines it with a weighted prediction model using team stats, pitcher matchups, and a dash of celestial influence. Built in Go and runs on a Raspberry Pi. Check it out on GitHub.
